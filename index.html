Ordering Process

  1. Open the Vorsy Pizza Homepage.
  2. Scroll to the Order Section.
  3. Fill out the order form (name, pizza type, size, quantity, address).
  4. System validates the inputs.
  5. Total price is calculated automatically.
  6. Order is added to the Orders List.
  7. Delivery is scheduled for selected date and time.
